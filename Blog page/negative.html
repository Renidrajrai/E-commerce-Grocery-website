NEGATIVE IMPACTS OF TECHNOLOGY

Many online stores claim to not have cash on delivery but have online payments and when they get the payment online they disappear or block them. So this is one of the negative impacts brought by the technology.

Has made us lazy:

Another negative impact of technology is that it has made us lazy and has made us too dependent upon technology. Many people don’t even go out of their houses for anything, if they need anything they can just tap it away and the food or anything that they want will be on their doorstep. Due to the fact that they don’t leave their house, it has also made them move less which has caused them to be unhealthy. This has also caused a bit of social awkwardness in people and later having problems even talking with people, so on top of making us lazy it has also made us unhealthy and socially awkward so technology is not all good if we look it in that way.
